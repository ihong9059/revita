- LoRa: 922.1 MHz, **SF12**, BW125kHz, CR4/6, 14dBm
- Tower uart0 (RS485): 115200bps, DE=P0.17, RE#=P0.21
- Tower uart1 (debug): 115200bps, TX=P0.16, RX=P0.15
- Link uart1 (debug): 115200bps, TX=P0.16, RX=P0.02
- MCP23017: I2C 0x20, GPA3=MUX_SEL, GPA4=MUX_EN#
- Tower 동작: TX→RX(5s timeout)→1s sleep→반복
